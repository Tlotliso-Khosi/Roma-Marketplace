# Roma Marketplace (formerly NUL Marketplace)

**Roma Marketplace** is a web-based service platform currently under development, designed to connect local service providers with individuals in need of various everyday services. Initially focused on the **National University of Lesotho (NUL) campus**, the platform serves as a pilot for a broader deployment across the **Roma area**.

## 🚀 Project Overview

The aim of this project is to **digitally transform how services are requested and offered**, creating a centralized, easy-to-use system where:
- **Users** can find and request services such as laundry, food delivery, cab rides, haircuts, grocery shopping, and more.
- **Service providers** can register to offer their services to the local community.

The platform is similar to Uber but extended to a variety of day-to-day services tailored to student and resident needs.

## 📍 Current Stage

- ✅ **Frontend** design and structure are in place.
- ⚙️ **Backend** development (authentication, service management, and user roles) is ongoing.
- 💳 **Payment integration** and **database storage** are planned and partially implemented.
- 🛠️ The platform is actively being built and tested within the NUL community before wider Roma deployment.
