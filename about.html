About NUL Marketplace

Connecting student service providers with customers at the National University of Lesotho since 2023. Our platform makes it easy to find and offer services within the campus community.

Our Mission & Vision

We're on a mission to create a thriving ecosystem where students can easily connect, trade services, and support each other.

Our Mission

To create a seamless platform that connects service providers with customers within the National University of Lesotho, fostering a vibrant community marketplace that enhances campus life and provides economic opportunities for students.

Our Vision

To become the go-to platform for all service needs within the university, creating a self-sustaining ecosystem where students can easily find what they need and offer their skills, ultimately enriching the university experience.

Our Journey

The story of how NUL Marketplace came to be

January 2023

The Idea

The concept for NUL Marketplace was born from a student project aimed at solving campus service needs.

March 2023

Development Begins

Our small team started building the first version of the platform with basic features.

June 2023

Beta Launch

We launched a beta version to a small group of students to gather feedback and improve.

September 2023

Official Launch

NUL Marketplace officially launched to the entire university community.

January 2024

500 Users Milestone

We celebrated reaching 500 active users on the platform.
