Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a service provider?

To become a service provider, create an account and select "Service Provider" during registration. Complete your profile with details about your services, pricing, and availability. Once approved, your services will be visible to potential customers.

How do I report an issue with a service?

If you encounter any issues with a service, please contact us through the form on this page or email us directly at support@nulmarketplace.ls. Include details about the service, the provider, and the nature of the issue.

Is there a fee to use the marketplace?

NUL Marketplace is free for customers to browse and connect with service providers. Service providers pay a small commission on completed transactions to help maintain the platform.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot password?" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to create a new password.
